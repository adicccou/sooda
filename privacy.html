Privacy Policy

This page explains what information Sooda may collect, how it is used, and the choices users have. This is a product-facing privacy page draft and should be reviewed with legal counsel before final publication.

Effective date: May 20, 2026 Company: Sooda Contact: hello@sooda.app

1. Information We Collect

Sooda may collect information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, business details, inventory records, product data, storefront content, and sales activity entered into the platform.

We may also collect technical information needed to operate the service, including device, browser, usage, and diagnostic data.

2. How We Use Information

We use information to provide and improve Sooda, support users, secure the platform, process business workflows, maintain product features, and communicate important updates related to the service.

3. How We Share Information

Sooda does not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers and infrastructure partners strictly as needed to host, maintain, secure, or support the platform, or if required by law.

4. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Retention periods may vary based on the type of data and how the service is used.

5. Security

Sooda uses reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. However, no method of storage or transmission is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

6. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on applicable law, you may have rights to access, correct, update, delete, or export certain personal information. You may also request help or raise privacy questions by contacting us directly.

7. Third-Party Links and Services

Sooda may link to third-party services such as app stores, external websites, or social platforms. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies, not this Privacy Policy.

8. Children’s Privacy

Sooda is intended for business use and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate authorization.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the effective date shown on this page. Continued use of Sooda after changes take effect means you accept the revised policy.

10. Contact

If you have questions or requests related to privacy, contact us at hello@sooda.app.
